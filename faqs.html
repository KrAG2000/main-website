Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

How do I create my first workflow?

Navigate to the "Dashboard" after logging in and click on "Create New Workflow." Our intuitive drag-and-drop builder will guide you through the process. You can also start with a pre-built template from our library.

What integrations are available?

FlowGenius AI integrates with hundreds of popular business applications, including CRMs (e.g., HubSpot, Salesforce), marketing tools (e.g., Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign), communication apps (e.g., Slack, Microsoft Teams), and many more. Visit our Features page for a full list.

Account & Billing

How can I update my billing information?

You can update your payment method and view your billing history by logging into your account and navigating to the "Settings" section, then choosing "Billing."

What happens if I exceed my task limit?

If you exceed your monthly task limit, your workflows will continue to run, but you may be charged for overage tasks depending on your plan. We will notify you well in advance if you're approaching your limit. You can always upgrade your plan for more tasks.

Troubleshooting

My workflow isn't running. What should I do?

First, check your workflow's activity log for any error messages. Ensure all connected apps are authorized and credentials are up to date. If the issue persists, please contact our support team with details of the workflow and error.

How do I report a bug or issue?

You can report bugs or technical issues directly through our Contact Us page or by sending an email to support@flowgeniusai.com. Please provide as much detail as possible.
