Frequently Asked Questions

What is a "task" in FlowGenius AI?

A task is any single action performed by FlowGenius AI, such as sending an email, updating a record, or extracting data. Each step in an automated workflow consumes tasks.

Can I change my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time directly from your FlowGenius AI dashboard. Changes will be prorated.
